THE SPARK OF TISA

TISA began with three visionary students in the University of Nigeria, Nsukka in 2024, frustrated by unreliable tcampus transport. United by a passion for innovation, they envisisioned a solution blending technology and accessibility. Pooling their skills in tech, logistics and community engagement, they developed TISA's app-based platform for safe, affordable rides. Starting as a small project, TISA quickly gained traction among students and staff, addressing mobility gaps with real-time tracking and user friendly booking. Formed with grit and collaboration, TISA's foundation reflect's UNN's spirit of ingenuity, laying the groundwork for a transportation revolution that prioritizes convenience, safety, and community connection within the University and country at large.

TISA - vision for the future

TISA aims to redefine transportation at UNN and beyond, delivering seamless, eco-friendly mobility. We strive to provide reliable rides, reduce carbon footprints, and foster community connectivity through innovative technology. Our vision includes expanding regionally, offering on-demansd and scheduled services that empower users with convenience and safety. By integrating real-time GPS, sustainable vehicles, and a user focused design. As TISA embarks on its Journey, we're more than a transportation service - we're a movement born at UNN, driven by community concerns and innovation. TISA invites you to join us in transforming how we move, one trip at a time. Together, we will create a legacy of accessibility, empowerment, and progress, ensuring TISA remains a beacon of change for UNN and beyond.
